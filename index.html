The Data Science Africa monthly seminars run every 2nd Thursday of a month, from 1100 - 1200 hours (East African time or UTC + 3h) with two slots of 30 minutes each.
Are you a student, researcher or industry player working on a machine learning or data science solution to a problem relevant to the African context?
Are you interested in sharing your solution with the data science community in Africa?
Please choose for yourself a slot in our upcoming seminars.
